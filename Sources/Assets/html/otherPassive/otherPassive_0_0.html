Rental Properties

Overview:

Rental properties are real estate properties that are leased or rented out to tenants. They can include residential properties such as houses, apartments, and condos, as well as commercial properties like office spaces, retail stores, and warehouses.

Benefits:

  • Generate passive income
  • Build equity over time
  • Tax advantages such as deductions for mortgage interest and depreciation
  • Diversify investment portfolio
  • Potential for property appreciation

Considerations:

  • Property maintenance and management
  • Market fluctuations and vacancy rates
  • Regulatory compliance and legal obligations
  • Risks associated with tenant defaults or property damage
  • Financing options and mortgage terms
